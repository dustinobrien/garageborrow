# Borrow Confirmation — Liability Copy

Short copy that displays just above the "Take it home" button on the borrow confirmation screen.

---

## Standard items (drill, hand tools, camping gear)

> Heads up: tools are tools. Use it safely, and if anything goes sideways while you're using it — that's on you, not Dad. Cool? Tap below to take it home.

## Power tools / sharp / heavy / motorized items

> This one's got teeth (or speed, or weight). You're saying you know how to use it safely, and if something goes wrong, that's on you. If you've never used one before, hit pause and ask first.
>
> By tapping below, you're acknowledging Dad's not responsible for injury or damage from your use of this item.

## High-value items (trailer, log splitter, 3D printer, CNC)

> This is one of the bigger items, and it requires Dad's approval before going anywhere.
>
> When you do borrow it: you're using it at your own risk, you're responsible for getting it home and back safely, and if it breaks under your watch you'll make it right.
>
> Dad's not a contractor or rental company — just a guy with cool stuff. Use accordingly.

---

These should be displayed in a small bordered callout above the confirmation button. Style: warm-amber border, italic body text, slightly smaller than primary copy. Not threatening. Not a wall of legalese. Just a friendly "hey, real quick" acknowledgment.
